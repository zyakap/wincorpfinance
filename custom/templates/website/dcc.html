What is DCC?

The Dina U Control Centre

The Dina U Control Centre (DCC) is an independent credit compliance and debt management system that Wincorp Finance works with to ensure responsible lending practices and client financial protection.

DCC promotes financial discipline, helps manage debt obligations responsibly, and ensures that lending decisions are made fairly, transparently, and within regulatory requirements.

Our Commitment

Responsible Finance Principles

At Wincorp Finance Limited, responsible lending and financial compliance are not just policies — they are core to who we are and how we operate every day.

Principle 01

Risk Management

Comprehensive risk management policies identify, assess, and mitigate potential financial and operational risks, ensuring the safety of our clients' assets at all times.

Principle 02

Transparent Lending

All loan terms, interest rates, and repayment schedules are communicated clearly and honestly before any credit agreement is finalised.

Principle 03

Fair Credit Assessment

Credit decisions are based on objective criteria, ensuring every applicant is assessed fairly without discrimination or bias.

Principle 04

Debt Sustainability

We assess each client's ability to service debt obligations without financial hardship, promoting long-term financial wellness over short-term gain.

Principle 05

Regulatory Compliance

Wincorp Finance strictly adheres to all relevant financial regulations and reporting requirements, ensuring full legal compliance in all credit operations.

Principle 06

Client Support

Clients experiencing financial difficulty can access dedicated support, restructuring options, and financial guidance to help them navigate challenges responsibly.
