Loan Application Process

Customer visits your website for information.
Customer registers and completes personal information.
Account activation after vetting, ensuring good credit ratings.
Customers apply with a click and follow the software's guidance.
LoanMasta performs automated loan requirements checks.
Loan decision (Approve or Decline) is made, and customers are notified automatically.
Approved loans are moved to a funding list, and alerts are sent for further funding decisions.
Once funded, the loan becomes a running loan in the system.
All document uploads are automatically labeled and filed.

Running Loans Management

LoanMasta ensures seamless management of running loans with automated reminders, updates, and default notices. Customers can view and download their loan statements, and staff can upload bank statements to update repayments.
